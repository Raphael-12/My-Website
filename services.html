How to treat various skin problems using bio products

  1. Stretchmarks

    2. This skin problem requires the use of bio Orange Extract Vitamin C Serum, 6 in 1 Soap, and the Skin Perfect Body Milk with tomato extract from the bio natural skin care pack.

    DIRECTION OF USE:

    During the day: wash affected area with the 6 in 1 bio soap, mix 2 drops of the Orange Extract Vitamin C Serum with a considerable amount of the skin Perfect Body Milk on your palms and rub affected area.
    At night: wash affected area with the 6 in 1 bio soap then apply 2 drops of the Orange Extract Vitamin C Serum on the affected area.

  2. Acne

    3. For this particular skin problem, you will need the bio 6 in 1 soap, skin toner and the restorative night cream for its treatment.

    DIRECTION OF USE:

    During the day: wash the face with the 6 in 1 bio soap then rub the skin toner on your face (using a cotton wool by preference).
    At night: repeat the same process as for the day and later on rub your face with the restorative night cream.

  3. Eczema

    4. The bio 6 in 1 soap and the skin toner is required for the treatment of this skin problem.

    DIRECTION OF USE:

    For this skin problem, just wash (Day and Night) the affected area with the bio 6 in 1 soap and then apply the bio skin toner on the area of the skin with eczema.

  4. Green Veins

    5. This skin problem requires the use of the bio skin toner, the Orange Extract Vitamin C Serum, and the restorative night cream.

    DIRECTION OF USE:

    Clean the affected area with the skin toner after bath (Day and Night) then mix a few drops (2-3 drops) of the Orange Extract Vitamin C Serum with the restorative night cream in your palms and rub on affected areas.

  5. Dry hands

    6. Only the restorative night cream is required for this treatment.

    DIRECTION OF USE:

    moisturize your hands daily by rubbing the restorative night cream on them every night.

  6. Sunburn

    7. For this skin problem, a consistent day and night treatment is needed using the bio skin toner, the Skin Perfect Body Milk, the orange extract vitamin c serum, and the Lovely essence 2 in 1 scrub.

    DIRECTION OF USE:

    During the day: First scrub the affected areas using the Lovely essence 2 in 1 scrub, then rub the skin toner on the affected areas before applying the Skin Perfect Body Milk with tomato extract on the body.
    At night: mix 2 drops of the orange extract vitamin c serum with a quantity of the Skin Perfect Body Milk on your palms and rub on affected areas only.

  7. Rides and Cernes

    8. A treatment for this skin problem requires the use of the bio restorative night cream.

    DIRECTION OF USE:

    Rub the restorative night cream around the eyes and neck. Do this every night till you see the results.

  8. Even skin color, fade dark spots and white spots

    9. These skin problems require the 6 in 1 bio soap, the bio skin toner, the Skin Perfect Body Milk, the orange extract vitamin c serum, and the restorative night cream.

    DIRECTION OF USE:

    During the day: Wash your body with the bio soap, apply skin toner and then apply the Skin Perfect Body Milk with tomato extract on the body.
    At night: Every 3 times per week, mix 2-3 drops of the orange extract vitamin c serum with a quantity of the restorative night cream on your palms and rub on the face, neck and arms.
    Every other night mix the orange extract vitamin c serum (2 drops) with the Skin Perfect Body Milk in your palms and rub on the affected areas/body.
How to treat various hair problems using bio products

  1. Hair breakage, Hair dandruff, and Hair baldness

    2. For the above hair problems, you will require the use of the bio hair soap and the aloe-vera hair moisturizer.

    DIRECTION OF USE:

    Wash your hair 2 times in a month with the bio hair soap.
    Put 1 teaspoon of the aloe-vera hair moisturizer in 100mls of water in a spray bottle and spray on your hair 5 times per day (shake the spray bottle before use).
    In case of hair baldness, massage affected area while spraying the aloe-vera hair moisturizer on the head.

  2. Hair volume

    3. For this hair problem, you will require the use of the bio hair soap, aloe-vera hair moisturizer, and the rice leave in hair conditioner.

    DIRECTION OF USE:

    Wash your hair 2 times in a month with the bio hair soap.
    Put 1 teaspoon of the aloe-vera hair moisturizer in 100mls of water in a spray bottle and spray on hair your 5 times per day (shake the spray bottle before use).
    After hydrating the hair with aloe-vera hair moisturizer, apply some quantity of the rice leave in hair conditioner on hair (Use the rice leave in hair conditioner 3 times per week).

  3. Hair Itching

    4. Its treatment requires only the use of the aloe-vera hair moisturizer.

    DIRECTION OF USE:

    Put 1 teaspoon of the aloe-vera hair moisturizer in 100mls of water in a spray bottle and spray on your hair 5 times per day (shake the spray bottle before use).

  4. Natural hair curls

    5. Its treatment requires the use of the aloe-vera hair moisturizer and the rice leave in hair conditioner.

    DIRECTION OF USE:

    Put 1 teaspoon of the aloe-vera hair moisturizer in 100mls of water in a spray bottle and spray on your hair and apply the rice leave in hair conditioner then twist and loosen your hair after 30mins - 1 hour.
