About Us

Sieyondji Akpovire Cynthia
Sieyondji Akpovire Cynthia resident from the country Cameroon in Africa founded the company BeautyInandOut in the year 2016. The company was created with objective to orient people on how they can feel great about themselves using natural cosmetics and through a fitness program. Cynthia says:"Due to the rampant growth of skin bleaching products in the cosmetic indusrty, from my observation, many users have suffered from side effects of the usage of these skin bleaching products such as the increase in acne, premature wrinkles and other skin problems."
We rose with solutions to these side effects with the use of natural cosmetics and to show how one can experience self-love with natural cosmetics. We target both men and women with health, skin and hair problems and provide our products that are suitable for their treatment. we also offer an online fitness program especially for women who wish to get back themselves after child birth without having to live in anxiety of weight gain.

Our Mission

BeautyInandOut provides natural Cosmetics for skin, hair and also provides body workouts routine to keep fit. Our products are made from the transformation of fruits and legumes that possess properties that provides benefits to the skin, hair and inner wellness.
Mission: We intend to provide awareness to everyone that nature heals and also to impact positively by improving your skin care and self Care routine with the help of our services.

Why choose BeautyInandOut

BeautyInandOut answers 4 questions: What, Why, When and How with regards to the health of your skin, hair and wellness and provides services suitable to your needs. What better place than BeautyInandOut where we transform fruits and legumes to natural cosmetics for a better and natural hair and skin care routine, where we provide fitness class for weight loss through diet and exercises and also where you can find natural teas to help maintain a healthy lifestyle. So, what better place to be to have a omplete self care and skin care routine to feel great about yourself.
